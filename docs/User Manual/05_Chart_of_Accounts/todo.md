# Chart of Accounts

- Add, view, edit, deactivate, and reactivate accounts (admin).
- Required account fields and validation rules (account number, duplicates, balances).
- Filtering, sorting, searching, and pagination on the accounts list.
- Account category and subcategory management.
- Account status rules (non-zero balance cannot be deactivated).
- Click-through to ledger from an account.
- Event log expectations for account changes.
