Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

If you have forgotten your password, you can reset it by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions sent to your registered email address to create a new password.

If you are able to log in and simply need to change you password, use the "Change Password" option in your profile.

How can I contact support?

You should contact your manager with any issues you may be having. If they are unable to assist, you can contact our support team by emailing support@finledger.com.
